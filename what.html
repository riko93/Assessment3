The application will benefit two main categories: Local Business owners: Local Businesses like cafes, restaurant and supermarket will be able to promote their business by showcasing the products that have a close to date expiry and/or fresh products that have a daily expiry date and have not been sold within the business hours. Customers: People will be able to purchase food at a greater discount due to the short term expiry and enjoy the food within the suggested date. Information Technology Department: This department will be responsible for implementations maintenance and allocation. Of the database and inventories Marketing Department: Data will be analysed and interpreted by this department to implement the product offered and retain users across the platform Customer service department: This department will deal with customer service management and guarantee that the communication between each part is smooth and efficient Shipping Department: Purchase information will be collected in an inventory snd retrieved every time that is needed
